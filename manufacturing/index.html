Manufacturing

A transparent process for export-ready agro products

Each stage is shown clearly so buyers understand how raw material becomes a packaged product ready for domestic or international shipment.

Step 1

Raw Material

Leaves and agricultural inputs are selected from suitable lots.

Step 2

Cleaning

Material is cleaned and prepared for controlled processing.

Step 3

Drying

Moisture is reduced with attention to color, aroma, and stability.

Step 4

Grinding

Powder products are milled to the required texture and grade.

Step 5

Quality Check

Batches are reviewed for buyer specifications and documentation.

Step 6

Packaging

Products are packed in food-grade, export-suitable formats.

Step 7

Export

Shipments are prepared with commercial and logistics support.
